About Me

My name is Juliette Mangon. I'm studying Computer Science and Mathematics with a minor in the Business of Entertainment, Media, and Technology at NYU, graduating in December 2025.

I am passionate about building technology that strengthens creative fields, with a particular focus on the music sector. I am interested in developing platforms that optimize how media is created, distributed, and experienced, and in designing algorithms and leveraging data for more precise segmentation, forecasting, and strategic decision-making.

I have experience building digital identities for artists, developing products from concept to production, and analyzing and visualizing music industry metadata to uncover patterns and derive insights that inform creative and business decisions.

I care deeply about the environment and aim to approach my work with sustainability in mind. I love to paint, spend time outdoors, and add fun features to my website (click on the square in the top right corner).
