About Me

My name is Juliette Mangon. I build products that strengthens creative industries. I'm interested in how we can leverage technology to shape the way media is created, distributed, and experienced. I approach that from two angles: building products that solve real problems for users in the media and entertainment, and exploring technology as a creative medium itself. In both, I prioritize strong logic and clean architecture.

Right now, I'm working on product and algorithms at Heritage, an app that helps people find experiences aligned to their interests and connect with like-minded people in NYC, and doing an AI and media strategy residency with Spinach Media Group. I also build websites for clients across the music, film, and creative space.

In December 2025 I graduated from NYU with a degree in Computer Science and Mathematics and a minor in the Business of Entertainment, Media, and Technology.

I care deeply about the environment and aim to approach my work with sustainability in mind. In my free time, I love to make art (usually painting) and add fun features to my website (click on the square in the top right corner).
