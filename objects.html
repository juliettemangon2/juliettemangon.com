A series of necklace collections that focus on texture, natural form, and how jewelry sits on the body.

Across collections, each piece is made of cut leather and beads. Each bead is either hand-formed in molten glass at the torch or carefully sourced from discarded objects and recontextualized within new designs.

01 / FERN

Exploring balance and contrast.

Jewelry design by Julia Majchrzak and Juliette Mangon.

Creative Direction and Photography by Juliette Mangon, Julia Majchrzak, Mila Baumann, and Nikolette Yarov.

JM

02 / TOTEM

Inspired by lichen, mineral layers, bark, and moss, each piece is assembled to highlight tactility, natural textures, and the beauty of irregular forms.

Jewelry design by Julia Majchrzak and Juliette Mangon.

Creative Direction and Photography by Amandine Mangon and Juliette Mangon.

03 / NYMPHAEA

Exploring blues and purples, inspired by winged animals and florals.

Jewelry design by Julia Majchrzak and Juliette Mangon.

Creative Direction and Photography by Juliette Mangon and Julia Majchrzak.
