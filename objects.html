TOTEM

This collection explores organic form and material history. Each bead is either hand-formed in molten glass at the torch or carefully sourced from discarded objects and recontextualized within new designs.

Inspired by lichen, mineral layers, bark, and moss, each piece is assembled to highlight tactility, natural textures, and the beauty of irregular forms.

Jewelry shoot photographed on disposable cameras in New York.
