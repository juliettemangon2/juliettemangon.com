About

I began building websites in early 2025 after noticing that many artists around me had strong bodies of work but no clear place to host them. What started as helping friends publish their work grew into client projects across art, film, and creative campaigns.

Often serving as the primary interface between the work and its audience, these sites are focused on translating each client’s identity into a responsive digital system.

Building websites has taught me how to iterate on design, balance functionality with aesthetics, and account for performance, media handling, and deployment details that matter in production.

Technical focus

Stack
Next.js, React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS
Tools
Figma, Cloudflare, Vercel, Github, Firebase, APIs
I develop websites by creating initial design concepts then translating them into code, then iterating on layout and functionality with client feedback, and handling deployment, maintenance, and ongoing updates.
