My favorite shot is the egg yolks and cigarettes going into the trash. I love this movie because it's funny at times, sweet at others, but also completely wild. Both main actors are amazing. MOVIE Parasite 11
Such a great story with amazing character development. This movie makes me anxious and uncomfortable in the best way. It feels like I’m always bracing for something bad to happen, and then it hits out of nowhere. The characters are so interesting, with traits that are both relatable and frustrating. Visually, it's beautiful too.
