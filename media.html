01 Choke Enough (Deluxe) Album
I love Oklou as an artist because her sound feels truly unique. It sits at an intersection of gentleness, hyperpop, and femininity in a way that feels very distinct. choke enough (Deluxe) is especially striking and might be one of my favorite Albums of all time. It is hard to describe, but her voice and the production feel incredibly delicate and and atmospheric, like being suspended in a light blue, cloudlike world.
02 Better Oblivion Community Center Album
Phoebe Bridgers and Conor Oberst's love child. What more could I ask for. This album is me, I am this album.
03 333 Album
333 is my go to Bladee album. However, I haven't listened to it in a few months, and I just got back into it again. Just as good as I remembered.
04 Solar Power Album
This may be an unpopular opinion but I love this album. Super good for the brunettes who like to hangout alone in the sun and feel optimistic.
05 Ivan Pokidyshev Artist
Ivan Pokidyshev is one of the first contemporary painters who really stuck with me. He uses thermography to photograph people and then translates those images into glowing painted figures. His work explores themes of science, life, love, and religion. Checkout his instragram here.
06 Laura Footes Artist
Laura Foote is a really inspiring painter for me. She paints entirely without reference, using only memory and imagination to create her work. Her paintings often feature human figures that are semi-transparent and abstract, blending into the surrounding space in a distinctive way. Just saw her exhibit at Shrine NYC! Checkout her instragram here.
07 Amy Renee Webb Artist
Her glass looking religious iconography is unreal. Also, she shares my love for neon green. Checkout her instragram here.
08 Lorenzo Amos Artist
His current work makes an effort to "capture the life of his surroundings". Generally, I really like his style and how his focus allows us to see similar backgrounds across paintings. Checkout his instagram here.
09 Love Lies Bleeding Film
My favorite shot is the egg yolks and cigarettes going into the trash. Kristen Stewart and Katy O'Brian play off of each other fabulously. At times, the timeline can be a little confusing, but you realize by the end that they are not very concerned with delivering a realistic storyline.
10 One Battle After Another Film
My favorite 2025 movie. Yay best picture.
11 Naissance des Pieuvres Film
A synchronized swimming movie about a french brunette who likes girls. Come on. Just watched this, I really really enjoyed it. I was the captain of my synchronized swimming team in highschool by the way.
12 Anatomy of a fall Film
Wow I can't believe I hadn't seen this until this week. SO good.
13 High Fidelity TV
I wish this show had lasted more than one season. Rob feels like Carrie Bradshaw’s cooler, more esoteric cousin. She is self-centered, hyper-introspective, and defined by an obsession with records. I cannot help but love her (I may be biased because I love Zoë Kravitz), and I want to live in her apartment.
14 Schitt's Creek TV
Schitt's creek is probably the funniest show I've ever watched, and it is comedic in such an intelligent way. I actually laugh out loud so often. I was surprised by how visually pleasing it is too.
15 The L word Generation Q TV
Just finished this. Extremely surprised at how much I liked it. This is also criticism for the O.G. L word but can we see character growth why do they keep making the same mistakes!? Good other than that.
16 The Bold Type TV
Focuses on 3 girls in their mid twenties who work at a women's magazine in NYC. This is my current watch. Super dramatic, love it.
